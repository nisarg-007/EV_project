# Washington Laws and Incentives (AFDC Summary)

## State Incentives
- **EV Charger Grants:** Level 2 EV charger grants for public, fleet, workplace, and multifamily housing. Up to $10,000 per plug (max 10 plugs). Priority for environmental justice communities.
- **Commercial AFV Tax Credits:** Credits for new or used medium- and heavy-duty AFVs and fueling infrastructure. Credits up to 75% of incremental cost ($25k-$100k depending on GVWR).
- **Alternative Fuel Vehicle (AFV) Retail Sales and Use Tax Exemption:** Exemption for new or used passenger cars, light-duty trucks, and medium-duty passenger vehicles. (Referenced in RCW 82.08.809).
- **Low-Income EV Rebates:** Rebate programs specifically targeting low-income residents for the purchase or lease of EVs.
- **Fuel Cell Electric Vehicle (FCEV) Tax Exemption:** State sales and use tax exemption for the first 650 FCEVs (new or used). Up to 50% of cost for new vehicles. Expires June 30, 2029, or upon reaching the cap.

## Laws and Regulations
- **ZEV Sales Requirements:** Washington adopts California's ZEV standards, requiring increasing percentages of new vehicle sales to be zero-emission.
- **Fleet EV Procurement:** Requirements for state agencies to prioritize EV procurement.
- **Low Carbon Fuel Standard (LCFS):** Establishes a program to reduce the carbon intensity of transportation fuels.
