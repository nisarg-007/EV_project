# IRS Clean Vehicle Tax Credits (FS-2024-26, July 2024)

## Policy Overview
This fact sheet updates frequently asked questions related to new, previously owned, and qualified commercial clean vehicles under the Inflation Reduction Act (IRA).

## Key Provisions
- **New Clean Vehicle Credit:** Eligibility rules for the $7,500 tax credit for new vehicles. Includes updated income and price limitations.
- **Previously Owned Clean Vehicle Credit:** Rules for the $4,000 credit for used clean vehicles.
- **Qualified Commercial Clean Vehicle Credit:** Rules for commercial fleet electrification.
- **Income Limits:** 
  - $300,000 for married couples filing jointly.
  - $225,000 for heads of households.
  - $150,000 for all other filers.
- **Price Limits (MSRP):**
  - $80,000 for vans, SUVs, and pickup trucks.
  - $55,000 for other vehicles.
- **Battery Requirements:** Detailed rules on critical mineral and battery component sourcing from the US or trade partners.
