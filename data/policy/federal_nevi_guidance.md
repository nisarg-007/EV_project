# National Electric Vehicle Infrastructure (NEVI) Formula Program Guidance

## Program Overview
The NEVI Formula Program, established by the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act (IIJA), provides funding to States to strategically deploy EV charging infrastructure and establish an interconnected network.

## Key Guidance Updates (Interim Final Guidance - Aug 13, 2025)
- **State Plan Simplification:** Minimizes content required in State plans to strictly statutory and regulatory requirements.
- **Corridor Flexibility:** Provides States with flexibility to determine appropriate distances between stations along alternative fuel corridors (AFCs).
- **Interconnection:** Minimizes requirements for grid integration and renewable energy considerations unless strictly required by regulation.
- **Site Hosting:** Encourages locations where the charging station owner is also the site host to accelerate delivery.
- **System Build-out:** Once a state's corridor system is determined to be "built out," NEVI funds can be used for public roads statewide.
- **Buy America:** Includes specific requirements and waivers for EV charger components to support domestic manufacturing.
